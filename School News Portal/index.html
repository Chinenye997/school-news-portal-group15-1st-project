ENSG- students win Reginal Competition

Published: February 25, 2026

A team of 10 Students participated in the South East Regional Olympiad hosted across the five south eastern states of Nigeria.

Over 900 students registered across Abia, Anambra, Ebonyi, Enugu and Imo States with over 200 students participating in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics). Overall, 10 of our students participated actively and won 4 Gold medals,4 Silver and 2 bronze medals

The overall best school winners are as follows :
1st Position:Federal Government College Enugu, Enugu State
2nd Position:City comprehensive Girls' College Akwa, Anambra State
3rd Position:Holyfield International School Owerri, Imo state

Enugu State Commissioner of Education visits Our School

Published: February 24, 2026

The Commissioner of Education Barrister Philip Odoh has commended our School for coming top in the just concluded West African senior School certificate Examination (WAEC) 2025.

90 students out of 100 students who sat for the examination had all A's in the following eight subjects namely Mathematics, English, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Economics, Government and Agriculture

He gave this commendation when he visited our school principal on the 4th day of February, 2026
